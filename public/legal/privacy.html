Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Owed is a UK benefits entitlement checker operated from the United Kingdom. Contact us at hello@owed.co.uk.

What data we collect

When you complete an assessment we collect: employment status, income, housing situation, number of children, disability status, age, and UK residency status. If you request a specialist callback we also collect your name, phone number, and optionally your email address.

How we use your data

Assessment data is used solely to generate your benefits estimate. It is not stored beyond your current session unless you opt in to a specialist callback. If you opt in, your details are shared with a vetted welfare rights specialist who will contact you directly.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Contact us at hello@owed.co.uk to exercise these rights.

Cookies

Owed does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Owed constitutes acceptance of any changes.
