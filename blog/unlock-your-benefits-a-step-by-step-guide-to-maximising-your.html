Introduction

As a UK resident, you may be eligible for various benefits that can help you cover living expenses, pay off debts, or even save for the future. However, navigating the complex benefits system can be overwhelming, and many people miss out on benefits they're entitled to. In this guide, we'll walk you through the types of benefits you may be eligible for, how to check your entitlement, and the benefits of using a benefits optimisation service.

Types of Benefits You May Be Entitled To

There are numerous benefits available to UK residents, including:

* Tax credits: Help with childcare costs, working tax credit, or child tax credit * Housing benefits: Assistance with rent or council tax * Council tax reduction: Discounts on your council tax bill * Universal credit: A monthly payment to help with living expenses * Disability benefits: Support for those with disabilities or illnesses

How to Check Your Benefits Entitlement

To check your benefits entitlement, you can:

* Use the Government's Benefits Calculator: An online tool that helps you determine which benefits you may be eligible for * Contact the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP): Reach out to the DWP directly to ask about your entitlement * Visit a local Citizens Advice Bureau: Get free, impartial advice from a local expert
