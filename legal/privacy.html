Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Owed ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use owed-app.vercel.app.

What information we collect

When you use our benefits assessment tool, you provide information about your employment status, income, housing situation, family circumstances, disability status, age, and UK residency. This information is used solely to generate your personalised benefits assessment.

How we use your information

Your information is processed in real time to generate your assessment. We do not store your personal details beyond your current session. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Payment information

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your payment card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Contact us at hello@owed.co.uk.

Contact

For privacy questions contact hello@owed.co.uk
